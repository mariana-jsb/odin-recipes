Grilled Veggie Sandwich

A Garden Fresh Delight

The Grilled Vegetable Sandwich is a delectable, veggie-packed delight featuring roasted zucchini, bell peppers, eggplant, and red onion. These grilled veggies are paired with pesto sauce and mozzarella cheese, making for a flavorful and satisfying vegetarian option, all served in warm ciabatta bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into quarters
  • 1 red onion, sliced into thick rings
  • 1 eggplant, sliced into rounds
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Ciabatta bread or your choice of bread
  • Pesto sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Roasted red pepper strips
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Butter or olive oil for grilling

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, and eggplant with olive oil, salt, black pepper, and dried oregano.
  3. Grill the vegetables until they have grill marks and are tender, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove from the grill and set aside.
  4. Slice the ciabatta bread into sandwich-sized portions and cut them in half.
  5. Spread a generous amount of pesto sauce on the inside of each piece of bread.
  6. Layer the grilled vegetables on one half of the bread.
  7. Add mozzarella cheese slices, fresh basil leaves, and roasted red pepper strips on top of the vegetables.
  8. If desired, drizzle a bit of balsamic glaze over the ingredients.
  9. Heat a panini press, grill pan, or skillet over medium heat.
  10. Brush the outside of the sandwich with butter or olive oil.
  11. Grill the sandwich for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is crispy, and the cheese is melted.
  12. Enjoy!
