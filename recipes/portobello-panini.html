Portobello Panini

A Smoky Mushroom Melt

This gourmet Panini is a mouthwatering sandwich featuring grilled Portobello mushrooms, caramelized onions, bell peppers, and chipotle sauce. It's a savory delight that offers a rich umami flavor with a touch of smokiness and spice, served on perfectly pressed ciabatta bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 ciabatta rolls or your choice of bread
  • 1/4 cup chipotle sauce (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 4 slices of provolone cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or a grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Toss the sliced portobello mushrooms, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Grill the vegetables for 5-7 minutes or until they are tender and have nice grill marks. Set them aside.
  4. Slice the ciabatta rolls in half horizontally.
  5. Spread a generous amount of chipotle sauce on one side of each roll.
  6. Layer a slice of provolone cheese on the other side of each roll.
  7. Place the grilled portobello mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions on top of the cheese.
  8. Heat a panini press, grill pan, or a large skillet over medium heat.
  9. Place the assembled paninis on the press or pan amd grill for about 4-5 minutes, or until the bread is crispy, and the cheese is melted.
  10. Enjoy!
