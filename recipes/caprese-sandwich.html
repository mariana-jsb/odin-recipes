Caprese Sandwich

A Mozzarella Marvel

The Caprese Sandwich is a classic that combines the freshness of ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil leaves, all drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze. It's a simple yet elegant sandwich that celebrates the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean.

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 8 slices of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 4 ciabatta rolls or baguette sections
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Slice the ciabatta rolls or baguette sections in half horizontally.
  2. On the bottom half of each roll, layer the fresh mozzarella slices, followed by the tomato slices and fresh basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle the extra-virgin olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the ingredients.
  6. Serve the Caprese sandwiches immediately, or you can optionally grill them in a panini press or grill pan for a warm, melty version.
  7. Enjoy!
